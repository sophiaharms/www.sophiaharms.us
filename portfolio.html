Discrete Response to Free Text

In advance of Super Tuesday, a client sought a form response that would be both standardized and centralized. They needed to be able to query results in the CRM, and also in their analytics sync. My solution pushes selected values into a hidden free text field and prepends each with a form-specific string. The responses that follow allow for a simple query like:

SELECT * FROM ContactsNotes
WHERE NoteText CONTAINS 'SuperTuesday_VA'

Hiking Map

Ahead of my February 2020 trip to Torres del Paine, I made the map that I then followed on the trek. I processed digital elevation model tiles from the JAXA ALOS Global Digital Surface Model to create the topographic map. For the elevation profile, I used GPS datasets collected by other hikers.
